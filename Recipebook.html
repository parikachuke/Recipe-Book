Avocado Salad Recipe

S.N Ingredients
a Diced Avocado
b English Cucumber
c Cherry Tomatoes
d Thick slices of Radish
e Thick slices of red Onions
f Teaspoons of minced jalapeno pepper
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: 3 cups diced avocado, ¾" dice (about 3 large avocados)

Step 2: 2 cups English cucumber, quartered, ¼" thick slices

Step 3: 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved

Step 4: ½ cup radish, halved, cut into ⅛" thick slices

Step 5: ½ cup red onion, 1" long, ⅛" thick slices

Step 6: 2 teaspoons minced jalapeno pepper, optional

Pasta Salad Receipe

S.N Ingredients
a Dried pasta like fusilli, penne, rotini or farfalle
b Sliced bell pepper
c Thinly sliced zucchini
d Halved cherry tomatoes
e Thinly sliced scallions
f Sliced pepperonicini or banana peppers
g Halved mixed olives
h Grated parmesan cheese or hard cheese
i Chopped mozzarella balls
j Parsley or basil
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: 1 pound dried pasta like fusilli, penne, rotini or farfalle

Step 2: 1 cup sliced bell pepper (1 medium)

Step 3: 1 cup thinly sliced zucchini (1/2 medium)

Step 4: 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes

Step 5: 1/3 cup thinly sliced scallions (5 to 6)

Step 6: 1/4 cup sliced pepperoncini or banana peppers, optional

Step 7: 1 cup (4 ounces) halved mixed olive

Step 8: 1 cup (2 ounces) grated parmesan cheese or hard cheese

Step 9: 1 cup (6 ounces) fresh mozzarella balls, chopped

Step 10:1/3 cup fresh parsley or basil, optional
